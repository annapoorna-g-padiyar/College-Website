Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT), Nitte Karkala, established in 1986 is approved by the All-India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi is a constituent college of Nitte (Deemed to be University), Mangaluru. All the eligible undergraduate programs are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi under the TIER-I category. Nitte University is accredited with ‘A+’ grade by NAAC and is ranked in the band of 101-150 in NIRF 2023. It is also in the rank-band of 451-500 of QS Asia University Rankings 2023 and 201-300 band in the Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Ranking 2022. NMAMIT,Nitte Karkala has active collaborations with Penn State University, University of Miyazaki, Ritsumeikan University (Japan) for faculty and student exchanges, research, internships and placements. Nitte DU offers undergraduate & postgraduate engineering programs and MCA at NMAMIT, Nitte Karkala campus. The institute has highly qualified faculty members providing guidance to the students. The college offers 100% placement assistance to the students. The highest package offered at NMAMIT, Nitte Karkala was INR 52.63 LPA. Some of the Top Recruiters and Corporates who have been assisting NMAMIT, Nitte Karkala through recruitment over the years are Oracle, Sony India, Robert Bosch, Microsoft, Amazon, Samsung Semiconductor, JSW Group, Infosys, TCS, Capgemini, Cyient, Mercedes Benz and many more.
